Historia

Welcome to Medellín, the city of eternal spring! Located in the heart of the Aburrá Valley in Colombia, Medellín has evolved from being an industrial center to becoming a vibrant tourist and cultural destination. Known for its temperate climate throughout the year and surrounded by impressive mountains, this city captivates its visitors with its rich history, modern architecture, and innovative spirit.
