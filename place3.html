PLAZA BOTERO

About

The Plaza Botero is a must-visit cultural destination in the city of Medellín, Colombia. Located in the heart of the historic center, this square is famous for hosting a prominent collection of monumental sculptures created by the renowned Colombian artist Fernando Botero. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely stroll while admiring the 23 massive and voluptuous bronze sculptures depicting human figures and animals. The plaza itself is a spacious and open space, surrounded by historic buildings and local shops, making it a lively and vibrant meeting point for locals and tourists alike. In addition to appreciating Botero's iconic works, visitors can also explore nearby art galleries and museums to further immerse themselves in the city's rich art and cultural scene. The Plaza Botero is a must-stop for art lovers, culture enthusiasts, and photography aficionados, offering a fascinating glimpse into Colombia's aesthetic and artistic legacy.
