PARQUE EXPLORA

About

In Medellín, Colombia's Explora Park, visitors have the opportunity to participate in a variety of exciting and educational interactive activities. From exploring biodiversity exhibits to immersing themselves in a fascinating aquarium, the park offers a range of captivating experiences. Tourists can enjoy interactive scientific experiments, shows in the digital theater and the planetarium, as well as participate in outdoor activities that promote scientific outreach and environmental preservation. In summary, Explora Park offers visitors the opportunity to learn while having fun, with activities for all ages ranging from nature exploration to immersion in the world of science and technology.
